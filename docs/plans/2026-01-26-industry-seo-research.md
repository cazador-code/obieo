# Industry SEO/AEO/GEO Research

**Date:** January 26, 2026
**Purpose:** Research findings for creating authoritative industry hub pages

## Executive Summary

### Key Trends Across All Industries

1. **AI Search is Reshaping Discovery**
   - 60% of searches now end without a click (zero-click searches)
   - AI Overviews appear in 16% of Google desktop searches
   - Gartner predicts 25% drop in traditional search volume by 2026
   - ChatGPT: 300M+ weekly users; Perplexity: 100M+ queries/week

2. **Local SEO Remains Critical**
   - 97% of consumers use internet to find local services
   - Google Business Profile signals account for 36% of local pack rankings
   - 76% of local searchers visit a business within 24 hours
   - Reviews are the #1 differentiator in local pack competition

3. **GEO Optimization is the New Frontier**
   - Top GEO tactics (citing sources, statistics, quotations) improve AI visibility 30-40%
   - 93% of AI Mode searches end without a click
   - Only 11% of domains are cited by both ChatGPT and Perplexity
   - Topic targeting over keyword targeting is the 2026 trend

## Industry-Specific Pain Points & Opportunities

### 1. Roofing

**Pain Points:**
- Seasonal demand spikes 300-500% after storms
- Storm chaser competition during peak seasons
- High-ticket sales cycle ($8,000-$25,000 per job)
- Insurance claim complexity and keywords
- 87% of homeowners research online before calling

**High-Value Keywords:**
- "emergency roof repair near me"
- "does [insurance company] cover wind damage roof [state]"
- "how much hail damage for insurance to replace roof"
- "roofing contractor near me" / "roofer near me"
- "roof replacement cost [city]"

**AEO/GEO Opportunity:**
- Storm damage preparation guides
- Insurance claim process explanations
- Roofing material comparison content
- Local weather pattern + roofing content

**Key Stat:** One Texas roofing company saw 109% traffic increase and 340% more leads with consistent SEO/content.

## Authority Page Content Structure Recommendations

Based on research, each authority page should include:

### Required Sections
1. **Comprehensive Industry Overview** (500-800 words)
   - What the service is
   - Why it matters
   - Industry trends and changes

2. **Common Pain Points & Solutions** (800-1200 words)
   - 5-8 major challenges business owners face
   - Practical solutions for each
   - Statistics to support claims

3. **SEO/AEO/GEO Specific Guidance** (600-1000 words)
   - How customers search for this service
   - Keyword categories to target
   - Local SEO best practices
   - AI search optimization tactics

4. **Statistics & Data Section** (300-500 words)
   - Market size and growth
   - Consumer behavior stats
   - Conversion and ROI data

5. **Comprehensive FAQ** (1000-1500 words)
   - 10-15 questions minimum
   - Schema markup for all Q&As
   - Direct, quotable answers

6. **Internal Links**
   - Link to conversion landing page
   - Link to related industries
   - Link to relevant blog posts

### Schema Markup Required
- FAQPage schema
- Service schema
- LocalBusiness schema
- BreadcrumbList schema
- Article schema

### AEO/GEO Optimization Checklist
- [ ] Lead with natural language headings
- [ ] Answer directly in first 40-60 words of each section
- [ ] Make sections standalone (extractable by AI)
- [ ] Use bullets, numbered lists, tables
- [ ] Include cite-worthy statistics with sources
- [ ] Add quotable definitions
- [ ] Include factual density (specific stats vs. vague claims)
